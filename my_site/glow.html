Glow

Once when we were out camping, I saw a weird shape in one of the benches at the picnic table. It looked like some one had set down some hot object and it had melted grooves into the bench. I think the object might have been part of a camping stove because of its shape. I thought the shape looked cool so I took a picture of it and drew over it in one of my art apps.
