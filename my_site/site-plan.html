Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my website is to be a place for me to mess around and explore the possibilities of web design and development. The website will include an about me section, a blog section, a gallery for any art or comics I might decide to put in, and individual pages for each image to be displayed full size. Kind of like a fusion of Deviantart and Tumblr that only has one person using it. It will also serve as home to my personal projects and experiments with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Audience

Myself and anyone else who might be interested.

Coconut Cream Custard
Makes 2 Servings
Ingredients
½ cup coconut cream
½ cup cream
2 tbsp sugar
1 beaten egg
¼ tsp vanilla
1 tbsp super tiny bits of shredded coconut, unsweetened
Preparation
Mix all the ingredients together very thoroughly.
Pour into a pan, turn heat to medium.
Stir constantly while scraping the bottom to make sure none burns.
When thickened and just barely beginning to bubble, remove from the pan and cool.

Ghost Room

GHOST ROOM
This is a room I had to make as part of an assignment for my Japanese class in high school. We had to draw a room with objects in different locations (right, left, front, back, behind, above, etc.) and then describe it in Japanese to a classmate who would then also draw the room. The purpose was to practice the words and grammar related to those positions, and by drawing the rooms we could see if we had used and understood them correctly and placed the items in the correct locations.

BUBBLES
This was my first attempt at drawing bubbles. I know now that bubbles need to be more transparent, and soap bubbles would have iridescence. Bubbles in water would not be as round as these ones when they first appear, and would wobble as they move, causing them to never be perfectly round.

Glow

GLOW
Once when we were out camping, I saw a weird shape in one of the benches at the picnic table. It looked like some one had set down some hot object and it had melted grooves into the bench. I think the object might have been part of a camping stove because of its shape. I thought the shape looked cool so I took a picture of it and drew over it in one of my art apps.
